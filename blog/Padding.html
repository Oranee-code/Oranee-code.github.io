What is Padding...

Padding is used to create space around an element's content, inside of any defined borders.
Adds space inside an element, between its content and border.
image is a gift box padding is like wrap around gift inside the box.

Padding - Individual Sides

For specifying the padding for each side of an element:

  • padding-top
  • padding-right
  • padding-bottom
  • padding-left
